Phaedra Concepts and Entities

Phaedra is designed to work with High Content Screening (HCS) data. It supports other domains that are related to HCS, such as Flow Cytometry, Kinetic Assays, High Throughput Screening and more, but the same terminology is used in all scenarios.

The objects that Phaedra works with, are structured into a parent-child hierarchy. For example, an experiment may contain multiple plates, but each plate belongs to exactly one experiment.
