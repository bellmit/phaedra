Dose-Response Curve View

The Dose-Response Curve view displays detailed information about the currently selected dose-response curve, well or compound.


The view consists of two panels: at the top is a graphical representation of the curve, at the bottom is a table listing the properties of the curve.

The curve is interactive, meaning you can click on a sample point to select that well. The well will be highlighted in other views, such as heatmaps. In addition, you can right-click on a point to view other aspects of the well, such as its image.
