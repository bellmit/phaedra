Chart Settings - Zooming, Selection, Panning

Selection, zooming and panning are options that are available on all the charts. However the way these options work can be slightly different from chart to chart.

2D Scatter and Density Chart

To zoom, select or perform panning you have to be in the right mode. You can only be in one mode at a time. You can see in the menu what the current mode is:


Selection mode

 You can click and drag to perform selection on the chart. If you select something outside the chart the selection will be reset. The gray area that appears defines which data will be selected. The points within the gray area are the selected points. By pressing and holding Ctrl while dragging you can add a new selection to the previous selection.

Zooming mode

 In zoom mode you can zoom in and out. Zooming depends on the movement you make.
  • Zoom-IN: drag your mouse to a lower right position
  • Zoom-OUT: drag your mouse to an upper left position

If you want to reset the zoom to view the entire chart you can click the reset zoom button
. The reset zoom button also works on 3D charts.

Panning mode

 This mode is only useful when you have zoomed. You can click and drag to pan the chart around.
Note: You can pan outside the initial plot range.

To switch between the different modes you just click one of the icons mentioned in the table above.

3D Scatter Chart

Zoom and panning works differently in 3D Charts. You still need to be on the appropriate icon to perform these actions:
  • Panning: this rotates the 3D chart.
  • Mouse wheel scrolling: this zooms in on the chart.
